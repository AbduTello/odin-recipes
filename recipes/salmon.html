Salmon

Description

Classic New York-Style Cheesecake

Indulge your taste buds with this delectable and creamy Classic New York-Style Cheesecake. This iconic dessert features a velvety smooth filling, rich with the essence of cream cheese and a touch of lemon, nestled atop a buttery graham cracker crust. The cheesecake is baked to perfection, resulting in a luscious and heavenly treat that will impress anyone lucky enough to savor a slice.

Ingredients

  • 4 salmon fillets (approximately 6 ounces each)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • Zest of one lemon
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh dill, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh thyme leaves
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Lemon slices (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper or lightly grease it with cooking spray.
  2. Rinse the salmon fillets under cold water and pat them dry with paper towels. Place the fillets on the prepared baking sheet, leaving some space between each one.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, fresh lemon juice, lemon zest, minced garlic, chopped parsley, dill, and thyme. Season the mixture with salt and black pepper to taste.
  4. Pour the lemon-herb marinade over the salmon fillets, making sure to coat them evenly. You can use a brush or spoon to spread the marinade if needed.
  5. Allow the salmon to marinate in the refrigerator for about 20-30 minutes to enhance the flavors.
  6. After marinating, place the baking sheet with the salmon in the preheated oven and bake for approximately 12-15 minutes, depending on the thickness of the fillets. The salmon should be cooked through but still moist and tender.
  7. Once the salmon is done baking, remove it from the oven and let it rest for a minute or two.
  8. Carefully transfer the salmon fillets to serving plates. Garnish each fillet with a lemon slice for an extra burst of citrusy flavor.
  9. Serve the Baked Salmon with Lemon and Herbs alongside your favorite side dishes, such as roasted vegetables, rice pilaf, or a fresh green salad.
